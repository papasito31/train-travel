About Travel Buddy NL

Travel Buddy NL was created to make train travel in the Netherlands more affordable, social, and fun — especially for students, international travelers, and budget-conscious explorers.

Using the NS Group Ticket system, you can travel for a much lower price when you’re part of a group. Travel Buddy NL helps you match with people going the same way — so you can team up, buy group tickets together, and save.

Our Smart Matching System shows you exact matches first, then other same-day trips you might consider. Even if the times aren't perfect, there's always someone going your way. 🚆

Built with love by a student who was tired of paying €25 to cry in another city. ❤️

Thanks for being part of the journey. Let’s save together, travel smarter — and never ride alone. 💸
