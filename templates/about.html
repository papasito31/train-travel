About Travel Buddy NL

This site is created to help travelers in the Netherlands find train buddies to share group tickets with. It's a student-led project made for saving money, meeting new people, and making train rides more fun and affordable. The goal is to connect travelers with similar routes and schedules, especially those using NS trains.
